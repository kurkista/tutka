        What is this?

        salmi watches the Strait of Hormuz: is it open, and what does that mean — for oil markets, and for everyday life in Finland.

        Take seriously: Daily transit counts (IMF PortWatch, official), Finnish prices (Statistics Finland, pörssisähkö), Brent daily closes.

        Take as editorial: The Passability Index and its bands are our own published formula on top of that data — versioned and fully explained under "How is this computed?".

        Take as best-effort: Live ship AIS (terrestrial receivers — currently largely dark in the Gulf, mid-crisis), flights (OpenSky), news volume (GDELT, rate-limited), intraday quotes (unofficial). Gaps are shown, never hidden.

        A one-person civic hobby project on free public data. Not navigation or trading advice.

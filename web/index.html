            What is this?

            tutka watches Nordic/Baltic-Russia tension: how loud is it right now, and what does that mean for everyday life in Finland.

            Take seriously: Finnish prices (Statistics Finland, pörssisähkö).

            Take as editorial: The Tension Index and its bands are our own published formula on top of GDELT news data — versioned and fully explained under "How is this computed?".

            Take as best-effort: Live ship AIS and flights (Gulf of Finland/Baltic region), news volume/tone (GDELT, rate-limited). Gaps are shown, never hidden.

            A one-person civic hobby project on free public data. Not navigation or trading advice.
